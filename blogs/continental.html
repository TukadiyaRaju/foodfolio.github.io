CONTINENTAL

In College Campuses, The food which is toughest to find is Continental. We can easily find other cuisines like north and south indian food. There are many different types of continental food, but some of the most popular include Italian, French, and Spanish cuisine. Italian food typically features pasta, pizza, and seafood, while French cuisine includes rich, savoury dishes like coq au vin and ratatouille. Spanish food, meanwhile, is often very hearty and features dishes like paella and tapas. No matter what type of continental food you're looking for, there's sure to be something to suit your taste. The options that LPU serves you are:-

1- Dominos:- Yes!! LPU has Dominos inside its campus. It's in The Unimall. The good part is that it delivers by 2 am. So, If you have late-night cravings, Dominos is the best possible solution.

2- Canadian Pizza:- Canadian Pizza is another very good option for continental food. Moreover, you also get Buy 1 Get 1 Free offer on Fridays. So, Friday Haiiiiiii!!!!!!.

3- Veggie Grill- The above options are very good but are very far away from the hostels. Here enter ‘Veggie Grill’, Its is a goto solution for the students because it's closer to the Hostel and provides good quality food.

4- Oven Express- Last but not least, Oven Express is one of the best food outlets peasant in LPU. Not only continental, but it also serves almost all cuisines. So, don't forget to give it a try.
